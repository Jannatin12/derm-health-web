About Derm Disease

Derm disease is a skin disorder caused by fungi, germs, parasites, viruses and infections that can attack anyone. Derm diseases can attack all or part of the body and can endanger the patient's health condition if not treated seriously

Eczema

Eczema is a term related to inflammation of the skin. This disorder is also known as dermatitis. When it occurs, an allergic skin reaction can be characterized by the appearance of redness, rash, and itching.

More
Psoriasis

Psoriasis is an inflammation of the skin that causes the skin to become scaly, thickened, easily peeled, and sometimes itchy. Psoriasis usually appears on the skin on the knees, elbows, lower back, and scalp.

More
Impetigo

Impetigo is a contagious skin infection that is mostly experienced by infants and children. This infection is characterized by the appearance of red patches and blisters on the skin, especially on the face, hands & feet.

More
Herpes

Herpes can cause an infection characterized by dry skin, blisters, or open sores that are watery. Herpes simplex virus (HSV) & varicella-zoster virus (VZ) are types of herpes viruses that commonly attack humans.

More
Selulitis

Cellulitis is a bacterial infection of the derm & underlying tissue. This condition can cause skin redness, swelling, blisters. Cellulitis occurs on the derm of the face, arms, eyes, & stomach.

More
Measles

Measles or Chickenpox is an infectious disease caused by the Varicella zoster virus. This disease is characterized by symptoms in the form of a red rash filled with fluid that feels very itchy all over the body.
